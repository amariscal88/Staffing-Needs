Dashboard Navigation Guide

Core Focus

This dashboard is a real-time, cloud-synced command center designed to bridge HR headcount planning with clinical capacity forecasting. It empowers leadership to simulate hiring scenarios, identify bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions to safely meet future patient demand.

Tab 1: Global Macro Capacity

Evaluates your global baseline reality. It mirrors the exact data pulled from the Google Sheet (Active Roster + Signed Offers) to give you a reliable anchor of your overall staffing health, without any hypothetical overrides.

Tab 2: State Telehealth Router

Visualizes the cross-licensure matrix across your 30 telehealth states. Lets you model, edit, and simulate fractional state capacity allocations directly next to localized monthly demand curves.

Key Logic & Distinctions

  • Headcount vs. Functional FTE: The Headcount Waterfall tracks actual human bodies. The Adjusted FTE Matrix tracks patient-facing capacity. Do not multiply Headcount by ratio capacity directly.
  • Interactive Triggers: The "Adjusted Scenario Gaps" timeline dynamically listens to your sandbox inputs. Red alerts will visually clear out in real-time as deficits are filled.
  • Cloud Syncing: Your scenario plans are saved to the cloud whenever you click "Save Inputs". This allows multiple leaders to view the exact same strategic pipeline assumptions.
